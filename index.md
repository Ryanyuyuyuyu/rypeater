# Rypeater Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 12, 2026

Rypeater is designed to be a simple Apple Watch experience. We do not collect, sell, share, or track personal data.

## Data Collection

Rypeater does not collect personal data.

The app does not use analytics, advertising, tracking technologies, third-party SDKs, or a network connection.

## Local Preferences

Rypeater stores a small number of preferences locally on your Apple Watch, including:

* Whether onboarding has been completed
* 12-hour or 24-hour time display preference
* Haptic feedback preference
* Winding status visibility preference

These preferences stay on your device and are not transmitted to us.

## Audio and Haptics

Rypeater plays local audio files and optional haptic feedback to simulate a minute repeater-style chime. This does not involve collecting or transmitting data.

## Children

Rypeater does not knowingly collect data from children or any other users.

## Contact

For support or privacy questions, please use the public GitHub repository that hosts this page.

## Changes

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the same public URL.
